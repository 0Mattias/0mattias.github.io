DNS-CLAW.

Agentic AI coding assistant built in pure C that operates entirely over DNS. Encodes LLM conversations as Base32 TXT record lookups with AES-256-GCM encryption, supporting plain UDP, DNS-over-TLS, and DNS-over-HTTPS transports. Seven tool-use capabilities, approval prompts for destructive ops, multi-turn sessions, four provider backends.
