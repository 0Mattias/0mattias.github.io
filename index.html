demo in your browser

A real-mode x86 emulator with a step debugger. Fibonacci is loaded and assembled when the page opens; press Step and watch the registers move. The full build does real instruction encoding and stays private; the demo keeps the part that teaches.

The boards run Forgejo, a Sliver C2 for lab work, Velociraptor, Caldera, BloodHound, and a restic ring where each board backs up to the next.
